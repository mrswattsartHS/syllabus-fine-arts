Flashcards Review

Click on each card to flip it and reveal the answer.

What is Tenebrism?
A dramatic lighting technique using strong contrast between light and dark.
Who created the sculpture of David in the Baroque period?
Gianlorenzo Bernini
Which element of art deals with the lightness or darkness of a color?
Value
What innovative lighting technique is Caravaggio known for?
Tenebrism, the use of extreme contrasts between light and shadow.
Name one key characteristic of Modernist sculptor Henry Moore's work.
Use of abstracted, simplified, and exaggerated human forms.
What are two key characteristics of Baroque art?
Dramatic lighting effects and emotional intensity that engages the viewer.
What defines Modernist sculpture?
Abstract, simplified forms that break away from traditional realistic representation.
How does Le Corbusier's Notre-Dame-du-Haut show sculptural qualities?
Through its upswept roof shape formed using sprayed concrete.

Georges Pompidou Center:
To make the interior space larger and to add an interesting design element.
